## Strategies
The strategies currently implemented and used are:
- Hidden Single
- Hidden Pair
- Hidden Triple
- Hidden Quadruple
- Naked Single
- Naked Pair
- Naked Triple
- Naked Quadruple
- X Wing

More patterns can easily be implemented. More can be found here: (Strategies)[https://www.thinkgym.de/r%C3%A4tselarten/sudoku/l%C3%B6sungsstrategien/]
